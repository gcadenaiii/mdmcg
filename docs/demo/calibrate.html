📋 Calibration Instructions

  • Gyroscope: Place sensor flat and keep VERY still for 10-15 seconds (usually auto-calibrates)
  • Accelerometer: Slowly move through all 6 orientations shown below. Hold each position for 2-3 seconds
  • Magnetometer: Move sensor in figure-8 pattern, away from metal objects and electronics
  • System: Auto-calibrates when other sensors reach 3/3
