ABOUT THE BRAND AND PRODUCT (context for the design): MDMCG is a heart-monitoring wearable brand positioned as "the Apple of wearable health devices" — it wins a crowded market through radical simplicity, calm confidence, and trust, not feature count. Every design choice should feel inevitable, precise, and unhurried. The product is a reusable heart-magnetic (MCG) sensor pod — about 5 cm × 5 cm and 1 cm thick, roughly a squared, slightly thicker credit card. It sits inside an undershirt, held over the heart by an ultra-thin, breathable mesh retention zone. It captures cardiac signal closer to the heart than wrist devices, without daily adhesive electrodes. It bridges the gap between consumer wellness wristbands and clinical monitoring, and is on a defined path toward clinical validation and remote-patient-monitoring workflows. Claim discipline: the brand says "clinical-grade aspiration," never "clinical-grade" as a finished fact. Keep this language throughout — confident, but never over-claiming.
